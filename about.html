About

Gaius is an online resource for studying De Bello Gallico. It's focus on syntatical notes and historical context, rather than rote translation. Gaius is community driven - everyone is encouraged to contact our staff, and continue expanding Gaius as a classical repository. Students are both benefactors, and benefactees of Gaius. We hope to continue a global, shared intellectual network for aspiring classics students. Peace.
